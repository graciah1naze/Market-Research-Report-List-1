<p><p>X-ray detector cards are essential components in medical imaging and security systems, categorized primarily into dual-energy, single-energy, and multi-energy types. Single-energy detectors capture images at a fixed energy level, providing basic diagnostic information. Dual-energy detectors utilize two energy levels for enhanced contrast and improved tissue differentiation. Multi-energy detectors operate across a spectrum of energy levels, offering superior material characterization and detailed imaging capabilities, thus catering to diverse applications in healthcare and industrial sectors.</p></p>
