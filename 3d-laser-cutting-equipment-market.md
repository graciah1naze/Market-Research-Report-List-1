<p><p>The 3D laser cutting equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: Fiber Laser and Carbon Dioxide (CO2) Laser. Fiber lasers utilize a solid-state medium to produce high-intensity beams, ideal for cutting metals and delivering precision with minimal material distortion. CO2 lasers, on the other hand, use gas to generate beams efficient for cutting non-metal materials such as plastics and wood. Both technologies serve distinct applications, catering to industries like manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace, enhancing productivity and quality in cutting processes.</p></p>
