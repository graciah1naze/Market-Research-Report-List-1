<p><p>The wireless stylus pen market is primarily divided into two types: active and passive. Active stylus pens feature built-in electronics, allowing for pressure sensitivity, tilt recognition, and customizable buttons, making them ideal for precise tasks like digital drawing and note-taking. In contrast, passive stylus pens, which lack electronic components, operate through capacitive touch and are generally less precise, serving mainly for basic navigation on touch screens. Each type caters to different user needs, from professional artists to casual device users.</p></p>
