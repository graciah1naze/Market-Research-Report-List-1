<p><strong>The HDMI Optical Transceivers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode Fiber</li><li>Double Mode Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HDMI optical transceivers market includes two primary types: Single Mode Fiber (SMF) and Multi-Mode Fiber (MMF). SMF is designed for long-distance transmission, utilizing a smaller core to support high bandwidth over extensive distances, making it ideal for applications requiring minimal signal loss. In contrast, MMF has a larger core that allows multiple light modes, facilitating shorter distances and higher data rates within localized environments. Both types cater to different needs in connectivity, speed, and distance for HDMI applications.</p></p>
